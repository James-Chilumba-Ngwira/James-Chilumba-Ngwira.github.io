3D & WebGL

Kazakhstan Virtual Museum

An interactive 3D museum experience built with Three.js, letting visitors explore Kazakh cultural artifacts, including a hand-modelled traditional dombra, inside a fully navigable WebGL environment set beneath a night sky.

  • Gallery pavilion modelled in Maya; artifacts sculpted and textured in Blender / Substance Painter, all exported to an optimized Three.js scene graph.
  • HDR environment lighting and ACES tone-mapping for a realistic, gallery-like atmosphere in the browser.
  • Orbit-based navigation with interactive object exploration to boost engagement and educational value.

Blockchain & ML

Immigration Management System

A secure, blockchain-integrated platform to modernize registration, verification and visa lifecycle tracking for international students, my final-year research project. Designed against a Supabase/Postgres schema (RLS, JWT auth); this live demo runs on a self-contained in-browser backend I built so it works without any external service.

  • Blockchain-hashed digital student identity with real HMAC-signed, QR-based, tamper-evident verification.
  • Role-based dashboards (Immigration Authority / Institution / Student) with live analytics, visa alerts, and student registration.
  • Full student self-service portal, visa renewal requests, contact info updates, institution messaging, and document downloads.
